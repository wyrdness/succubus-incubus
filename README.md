## Overview

Succubus (female) and Incubus (male) are demons from medieval Christian demonology that seduce humans during sleep, drain life force through sexual encounters, and sometimes steal semen or impregnate victims.

These entities are described as shape-shifters appearing as attractive humans, draining victims' life force through sexual contact. Medieval texts claimed they could steal semen from men or use it to impregnate women, explaining both nocturnal emissions and pregnancies out of wedlock.

Modern understanding attributes these experiences to **sleep paralysis with hypnagogic hallucinations**, though the phenomenon appears in folklore worldwide under various names including Lilith, Mara, and Alp.

### Historical Documentation

### Scientific Analysis
**Sleep Paralysis with Hypnagogic Hallucinations** ‚Äî Modern sleep medicine has thoroughly documented this phenomenon:
- REM intrusion into waking consciousness
- Temporary paralysis upon waking
- Vivid hallucinations (often sexual or threatening)
- Sensation of presence or weight on chest
- Well-established physiological basis

## Theories & Explanations

### Scientific Explanations (Accepted)

1. **Sleep Paralysis with Hypnagogic Hallucinations**
   Sleep paralysis episodes combined with vivid sexual hallucinations during REM sleep intrusion. Well-documented in sleep medicine research.

2. **Nocturnal Emissions Explanation**
   Medieval culture needed to explain natural nocturnal emissions and erotic dreams without acknowledging normal sexuality.

3. **Psychological Projection**
   Projection of repressed sexual desires, particularly prevalent in sexually repressive medieval Christian culture.

### Cultural/Historical Explanations

1. **Social Control Mechanism**
   Belief system used to control sexual behavior, explain illicit pregnancies, and enforce moral standards.

2. **Witch Trial Justification**
   Confessions of demonic sexual encounters used to prosecute accused witches (obtained through torture).

### Paranormal Claims (Not Scientifically Supported)

1. **Genuine Demonic Entities**
   Traditional belief: real demons that sexually assault humans during sleep.

2. **Astral/Spirit Entities**
   Modern occult view: non-physical entities from astral plane that feed on sexual energy.

### Current Understanding

**Scientific Consensus**: Succubus/Incubus experiences are **sleep paralysis episodes** with sexual hypnagogic/hypnopompic hallucinations.

**Well-Established Facts**:
- Sleep paralysis affects 7-8% of population
- Vivid hallucinations common during episodes
- Sexual content frequent in hallucinations
- Sensation of presence/weight on chest
- Temporary inability to move
- More common with sleep deprivation and stress
